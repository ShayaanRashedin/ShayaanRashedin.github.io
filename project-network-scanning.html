Network Scanning & Reconnaissance

Coming soon — this page is ready for your full write-up.

Objective

Map services on Metasploitable, identify versions/banners, and triage known CVEs.

Tools

  • Nmap
  • GVM/OpenVAS
  • Wireshark (optional)

Process (outline)

  1. Discover target IP and run full port scan with Nmap.
  2. Service/version detection and banner grabbing.
  3. Research exposed services for known vulnerabilities.
  4. Run OpenVAS scan to find CVEs automatically; export report.
  5. Prioritize findings and propose next steps.
